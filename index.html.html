COFFEE

Benefits of Coffee

  • Boosts energy and improves focus.
  • Rich in antioxidants.
  • May improve mood.
  • Helps increase physical performance.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cups water
  • 0.5 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoons instant coffee powder
  • 2 teaspoons sugar

Steps to Prepare

Boil water: Pour 0.5 cups water into a small saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat.

Add coffee & sugar: Add 1 teaspoons instant coffee powder and 2 teaspoons sugar to the boiling water. Stir well until fully dissolved.

Add milk: Pour in 0.5 cups milk and stir to combine. Heat over medium flame until it just begins to boil.

Serve: Pour into your favourite cup and enjoy hot!
